FAQs

Income Tax Calculator FAQs

Which assessment year does this calculator support?

It supports FY 2025-26 / AY 2026-27 slab comparison for resident individual taxpayers.

Does the calculator apply standard deduction?

Yes. If salary / pension is selected, it applies ₹50,000 under old regime and ₹75,000 under new regime, limited to the entered income.

Does the new regime allow all deductions?

No. The new regime allows only selected deductions. Use the new regime allowed deduction field only for deductions that are actually permitted for your case.

Is this a substitute for tax filing advice?

No. This is a quick estimate. For ITR filing, capital gains, business income, surcharge, foreign income or special income, consult a tax professional.
