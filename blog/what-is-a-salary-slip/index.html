What is a Salary Slip?

A salary slip, also called a payslip, is a salary statement that shows the breakup of an employee’s salary for a specific month or pay period.

Common Components

  • Company and employee details
  • Salary month and paid days
  • Basic salary, HRA and allowances
  • PF, ESIC, TDS and other deductions
  • Gross salary and net salary payable

Why It Matters

Salary slips help employees as proof of income and help employers maintain payroll records and salary transparency.
