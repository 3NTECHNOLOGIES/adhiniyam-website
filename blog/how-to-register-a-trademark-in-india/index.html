Quick Answer

Trademark registration generally involves choosing a brand name or logo, checking similar trademarks, selecting the correct class, preparing applicant details and documents, filing the application, tracking examination and responding to objections if raised.

What is a Trademark?

A trademark is a mark used to identify and distinguish goods or services of one business from another.

Why Trademark Matters

  • Brand protection
  • Business identity
  • Customer recognition
  • Long-term brand value

Basic Filing Process

Choose brand name/logo, identify goods or services, select class, conduct search, prepare applicant details, file application and track status.

Common Mistakes

  • Filing without search
  • Choosing generic names
  • Wrong class
  • Ignoring objections
