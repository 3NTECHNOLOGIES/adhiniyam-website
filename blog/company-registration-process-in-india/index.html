Quick Answer

Company registration generally involves choosing the right business structure, selecting a business name, preparing applicant and office details, arranging documents, filing the application and completing post-registration steps.

Common Business Structures

  • Proprietorship
  • Partnership Firm
  • LLP
  • Private Limited Company
  • Section 8 Company
  • MSME / Udyam

How to Choose a Structure

The right structure depends on ownership, liability, funding plans, compliance capacity, business activity and long-term goals.

Basic Registration Process

Decide structure, choose name options, prepare applicant details, arrange registered office details, prepare documents and file application.

After Registration

Open a current account, check GST applicability, set up invoicing, consider trademark protection and track compliance.
