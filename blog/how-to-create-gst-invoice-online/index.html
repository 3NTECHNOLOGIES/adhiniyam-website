What is a GST Invoice?

A GST invoice is a tax invoice issued by a GST-registered business for the supply of goods or services. It generally contains seller, buyer, invoice, item, tax and total value details.

Details Usually Included

  • Seller name, address and GSTIN
  • Invoice number and date
  • Customer details and GSTIN
  • Item description, quantity and rate
  • Taxable value, GST rate and tax amount

Step-by-Step Process

Enter seller details, add invoice number/date, add customer details, enter products or services, choose GST type and rate, review totals and download the invoice PDF.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Wrong invoice number
  • Incorrect GSTIN
  • Wrong GST rate
  • Missing customer details
  • Not reviewing totals
