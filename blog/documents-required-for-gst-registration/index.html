Documents Required for GST Registration in India

Know the common documents and details usually required for GST registration in India for proprietorship, partnership firm, LLP, company and other businesses.

Related Tool: Invoice GeneratorRelated Service: GST Registration & Filing
Quick Answer

Common GST registration documents may include PAN, Aadhaar, photograph, business address proof, bank details, email ID, mobile number, business activity details, authorized signatory details and entity documents.

In this guideWhy Documents MatterCommon Details RequiredEntity-Wise DocumentsCommon Mistakes

Why Documents Matter

Correct documents help avoid delays, clarification notices and rejection issues during GST registration.

Common Details Required

  • Legal name and PAN
  • Mobile and email
  • Business address and activity
  • Bank details
  • Authorized signatory details

Entity-Wise Documents

Proprietorship, partnership firm, LLP and company documents may differ. Companies and LLPs generally require entity documents and authorization details.

Common Mistakes

  • Wrong PAN
  • Incorrect address
  • Missing NOC
  • Unclear scanned documents
